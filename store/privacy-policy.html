Privacy Policy — Word Learner

Effective date: 27 April 2026

Summary

Word Learner is an educational app for kids learning the most common English words. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This page explains what we don't do — and the few minor things the app does locally on your device.

Data we collect

None. Word Learner does not collect any personal data, contact information, device identifiers, location, photos, microphone input, browsing history, or usage analytics — from you, your child, or anyone else.

Data stored on your device

The app stores two small preferences in your device's local storage:

  • Voice preference — which text-to-speech voice you've selected
  • Star count — how many words you've answered correctly in your current session

This information never leaves your device and is not sent to us or any third party. You can clear it any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings → Apps → Word Learner → Storage → Clear data.

Things the app loads over the network

On first launch, the app downloads:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) — used purely for visual styling of the letters. Per Google's policy, Google may log the IP address of the device making the font request. We do not receive or have access to this information. See Google Fonts FAQ.

After fonts are loaded, the app works fully offline. The app makes no other network requests.

Speech (text-to-speech)

The "🔊 hear it" feature uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine. Audio is generated locally and is never sent to any server. The list of available voices comes from your device's TTS configuration.

Children's privacy

Word Learner is designed for children, including those under 13.

Because the app collects no information from anyone, it complies with:

  • the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States
  • the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and GDPR-K in the European Union
  • Google Play's Designed for Families program requirements

The app contains no ads, no in-app purchases, no links to external websites, and no third-party SDKs other than the Google Fonts request described above.

Third-party services

The only third-party network call the app makes is to Google Fonts. We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Crash-reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Social-media SDKs
  • Login providers

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the "Effective date" above will change and the new version will be posted at the same URL.
