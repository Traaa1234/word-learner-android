Word Learner — the colorful way to read more.

Help your child master the 200 most-used words in English with bright, friendly flashcards, real human-voice pronunciation, and fun fill-in-the-blank challenges.

These 200 words make up roughly half of every page your child will ever read. Recognizing them on sight is the single biggest jump in early literacy — and Word Learner makes that practice feel like play.

🦊 TWO WAYS TO LEARN
• Flashcards — Tap through each word with phonetic respelling (e.g. "PEE-puhl") and IPA notation (/ˈpiːpəl/). Tap the speaker to hear it spoken by a real voice.
• Fill-in-the-Blank — Type the missing letters in a word. Get instant feedback, hear the word read aloud when you're right, and earn a star for every correct answer.

⭐ WHAT'S INSIDE
• The 200 most common English words, ranked by frequency
• Color-coded grammar categories: nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, pronouns, prepositions, determiners, conjunctions, and auxiliary verbs
• Filter to study just one category at a time (e.g. "All verbs")
• Phonetic spelling AND International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) for every word
• Pick your favorite voice from any English text-to-speech voice on your device — male or female, accent of your choice
• A friendly fox mascot that cheers you on
• A star counter that grows with every win
• Shuffle mode for review

📵 BUILT FOR KIDS — AND PARENTS
• No ads. Ever. No banners, no video, no sponsored content.
• No in-app purchases. What you see is everything.
• No tracking. No analytics. No data collection.
• No accounts. Just open and play.
• Works fully offline after the first launch.
• COPPA and GDPR-K compliant. Eligible for Play's Designed for Families program.

🎯 WHO IT'S FOR
Best for ages 5 to 10, but anyone learning English (including adult ESL learners) will find it useful. Older kids working on spelling will especially enjoy the fill-in-the-blank mode.

📱 Designed for both phones and tablets. Looks great on any screen.

Made with love for kids who are just learning to read. Bug reports, ideas, and "thank yous" are all welcome at lw_jen@yahoo.com.

Happy learning! 🌈

## Hosting the privacy policy on GitHub Pages (free, 5 min)

Play Store **requires** a public URL for your privacy policy. The simplest free path:

1. Create a free GitHub account if you don't have one (https://github.com/signup)
2. Click "New repository" → name it something like `word-learner-privacy`
3. Make it **Public**, check "Add a README"
4. Click "Add file → Upload files" and drag in `store/privacy-policy.html`
5. **Rename it to `index.html`** before committing (so the URL is cleaner)
6. Commit
7. Go to **Settings → Pages** → set **Source** to "Deploy from a branch", pick `main` branch and `/ (root)` folder, save
8. Wait ~1 minute. Your URL will be `https://<your-github-username>.github.io/word-learner-privacy/`
9. Paste that URL into Play Console's "Privacy policy" field

If you want a custom domain later, GitHub Pages supports it for free.
